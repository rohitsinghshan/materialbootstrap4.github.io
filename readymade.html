Food Ordering Script Features

Add Menu For Restaurants

Restaurants can add their menus with details like cuisine, name, description, image and price.

Delivery Address Management

Customers can save their delivery addresses. Ordering again, they will be offered to select the previous address.

Favorites

Customers can mark the food items they like as their favorites so that they can see them in their favorites list.

List/Filter/Sort Restaurants

Customer can view resultant list of restaurants over the site according to the search criteria they have entered.

Manage Opening Hours

Restaurant can turn off the restaurant’s status, then no customer will be able to order from the restaurant.

Menu Management For Restaurant

Restaurant can manage their menu. They can edit cuisine type, item name, price, description, item images.

Order Details

Restaurant can check the order details of the received order; customer name, contact number, delivery address.

Review Rating

Once the food is delivered then they can rate the restaurant and write a review about it.

View Received Orders

Restaurants can view the orders details like Order no., Order Status, Placed Date/Time, Order type.

View Menu Detail

Customer can choose a cuisine to view the food items that are available within & sort the food items by price & most

View Restaurant Detail

Customers can view restaurant details of the selected restaurant like restaurant name, Image, ratings,
